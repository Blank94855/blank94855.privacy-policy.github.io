Privacy Policy

Bot Name: Nova AI Bot
Purpose: AI assistant for Discord servers and Telegram chats

Your Privacy is Protected

We do NOT collect, store, or use your data for training. Your conversations with Nova are processed in real-time and are not permanently stored or used to improve AI models.

Data Collection

Nova AI Bot does NOT collect any personal data. Specifically:

  • We do not store your messages or conversation history
  • We do not collect usernames, user IDs, or profile information
  • We do not track your usage patterns or behavior
  • We do not create user profiles or analytics

AI Training

Your conversations are NOT used for AI training. The bot:

  • Processes your messages in real-time only
  • Does not send your data to train AI models
  • Maintains temporary conversation context only during active sessions
  • Automatically discards conversation history when sessions end

Data Processing

When you interact with Nova:

  • Your messages are sent to the AI service (Shapes.inc) for response generation
  • Messages are processed temporarily and then discarded
  • No permanent records of conversations are maintained
  • The bot maintains minimal temporary context for conversation flow only

Third-Party Services

Nova uses the following external service:

  • Shapes.inc AI API: Processes messages to generate responses. Please refer to their privacy policy for their data handling practices.

Platform Integration

As a bot for Discord and Telegram, Nova:

  • Only accesses messages where it's mentioned in Discord channels or Telegram chats
  • Does not monitor or log general channel or chat activity
  • Requires standard permissions on each platform to function
  • Follows the Terms of Service and Privacy Policy of each platform (Discord and Telegram)

Data Security

While we don't store your data, we ensure:

  • All communications use secure, encrypted connections
  • Temporary processing follows security best practices
  • No sensitive information is logged or retained

Your Rights

Since we don't collect or store your data:

  • There's no personal data to access, modify, or delete
  • You can stop using the bot at any time
  • You can remove the bot from your server if you're an administrator

Changes to Privacy Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will notify users through the bot's about command or platform announcements. Continued use of the bot after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or Nova's data practices, please contact the bot developer.

Summary

Nova respects your privacy completely. We don't collect, store, share, or train on your data. Your conversations are processed temporarily to provide responses and then discarded. Your privacy is our top priority.
